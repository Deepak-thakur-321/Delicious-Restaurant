MASTER CHEF

RANVEER BRAR

Chef Ranveer Brar, one of India’s most celebrated chefs, started his culinary journey at the age of 17 in the historic streets of Lucknow. Deeply inspired by the city’s rich Awadhi cuisine, he honed his skills under legendary street vendors before training professionally at the Institute of Hotel Management, Lucknow.

His journey took him across India, from the bylanes of Old Delhi to the coastal kitchens of Kerala, where he mastered the art of balancing traditional Indian flavors with modern techniques. Internationally, he gained experience in fine dining restaurants in Boston, London, and New York, bringing a global perspective to Indian cuisine.

Chef Ranveer has helmed some of India's finest restaurants, including the famous Banq in Boston, which was recognized for its unique fusion of Asian and Indian flavors. Back home, he has been the creative force behind renowned restaurants like TAG Gourmet Kitchen and has played a key role in promoting Indian street food globally.

Beyond the kitchen, he is a well-known TV host, judge, and content creator, inspiring millions through his shows and digital platforms. His approach to cooking is not just about recipes—it’s about reliving traditions, reviving forgotten techniques, and making Indian cuisine a global sensation.

From reimagining street food to presenting India's culinary diversity on world stages, Chef Ranveer Brar continues to shape the future of Indian gastronomy with his expertise, passion, and innovation.

“For me, food is an emotion, a story that connects us to our roots. Indian cuisine is not just about spices; it’s about tradition, culture, and the art of storytelling through flavors.”

MASTER CHEF

CHEF SANJEEV KAPOOR

Chef Sanjeev Kapoor is a name synonymous with Indian cuisine, revolutionizing home cooking and taking Indian flavors to the global stage. A Padma Shri awardee and one of the most celebrated chefs in India, he has dedicated his life to making cooking simple, accessible, and innovative for people across generations.

With an illustrious career spanning decades, Chef Sanjeev Kapoor has hosted the longest-running Indian TV cooking show, "Khana Khazana," inspiring millions to embrace the joy of cooking. His expertise extends beyond television—he is an entrepreneur, author of bestselling cookbooks, and a culinary ambassador who has represented India at prestigious international food events.

Passionate about innovation, he has redefined Indian cuisine through his restaurants, product lines, and digital platforms, making traditional flavors relevant in today’s world. From reviving lost recipes to crafting modern adaptations, Chef Sanjeev Kapoor continues to shape India's gastronomic landscape.

Beyond his expertise in Indian cuisine, Chef Kapoor is a strong advocate for **healthy and sustainable eating**. His culinary innovations blend nutrition with taste, ensuring that every dish is not just flavorful but also nourishing. Through his food ventures, he promotes balanced meals and wholesome ingredients that cater to evolving dietary needs.

Passionate about innovation, he has redefined Indian cuisine through his restaurants, product lines, and digital platforms, making traditional flavors relevant in today’s world. From reviving lost recipes to crafting modern adaptations, Chef Sanjeev Kapoor continues to shape India's gastronomic landscape.

His philosophy is simple: food should be a celebration of culture, taste, and wellness. Through his unwavering dedication, he has not only made Indian cuisine a global phenomenon but has also empowered home cooks and aspiring chefs alike.

“Food is not just about taste; it’s about memories, emotions, and the love that goes into making every dish.” — Chef Sanjeev Kapoor
